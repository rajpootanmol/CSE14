NEWS ABOUT THE CHAUHAN COMMUNITY - BELONGS TO RAJPUT CLAN

Chauhan, a name derived from the historical Chahamanas, a clan name associated with various ruling Rajput families in the present day indian state of Rajasthan from seventh century onwards. The Chauhans were historically a powerful group in the region now known as Rajasthan. In sanskrit Rajput means the "Son of king" and they are considered to be one of the most prominent castes in india. They come under kshatriyas and they have inherited characteristics of warrior in them. Which often reflects in their nature. Also, people of chauhan caste are known for being deeply rooted in their traditions and customs. Particularly when it comes to marriage they have certain customs and traditions. Chauhan community is known for its rich culture.
